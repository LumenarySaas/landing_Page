Get in touch

Is my money safe if Lumenary disappears?

Yes. Lumenary is fully non-custodial — your funds sit in a smart account that only your team's Passkeys can move. If Lumenary ceased to exist tomorrow, your stablecoins remain entirely under your control on-chain, and you can withdraw them directly.

Do I need to understand crypto to use this?

No. You fund your account with Canadian dollars over Interac and withdraw the same way. Lumenary handles the conversion, the on-chain mechanics and the tax paperwork behind a dashboard that reads like online banking.

Is this legal and compliant in Canada?

Your business is verified against Corporations Canada and Revenu Québec, AML obligations are handled by FINTRAC-registered partners, and your tax documents (T5, T1135, TP-21.4.39) are prepared automatically under the CARF 2026 framework.

What are the fees, and is there a lock-up?

During the private beta there is no platform fee and no lock-up — you can withdraw at any time. At general availability, premium is a flat CAD monthly subscription with a performance fee on yield earned; deposits and withdrawals are never locked.

How fast can I get my cash back?

Withdrawals up to $9,999 settle to your linked Canadian bank account by Interac in roughly two to four hours. Larger amounts settle by EFT or wire within one to two business days.
